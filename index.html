About Us.

As a local, family-owned business, we are committed to the "small store" atmosphere. We will always provide you with excellent customer service. Part of that outstanding service is conducting a Video Gait Analysis, a procedure that will help us determine what shoe is best for you by measuring your feet, watching your stride, and identifying any bio-mechanical inefficiencies. Then, you have fun choosing your shoe from over 40 models in our store.
Mike and Alison Rush started running in middle school and haven't looked back since. Mike continued his running career at the University of Arkansas and has coached at Fayetteville High School and local runners of all distances, from the 5k to the marathon. Alison also ran for Arkansas, finishing her senior year as the LadyBacks' first runner and qualifying for the Olympic trials in the 10K.
